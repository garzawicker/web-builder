A prescription for improving health in Colorado.

Our new Health Institute integrates 10 disciplines to improve lives, communities and careers.

Help underserved populations navigate access to health care. Lead the way in the transformative field of lifestyle medicine. Learn through experience at some of the state’s top health organizations. The road to health leadership and personal wellness runs through MSU Denver.

What’s possible when you reimagine higher education?

A thriving urban university that fosters student success through classroom-to-career connections, relevant degrees and excellent faculty.

Build and operate satellites at an innovative aerospace company. Make beer at a legendary brewery. Produce an Emmy-winning newscast. All without leaving campus. Plus, our downtown location provides unparalleled access to internships and networks that jump-start careers. Just ask this “SportsCenter” anchor.

The possibilities are endless.

Here, you can master your future.

Master’s degrees in business, health, teaching and technology designed to advance your career and community.

Advance your community – and career – with an advanced degree in a critical Colorado field: business administration, accounting, teaching, cybersecurity, health administration, human nutrition and dietetics, social work or clinical behavioral health. Level up in your current job or change careers with a flexible and relevant graduate degree designed with students in mind.

Teaching tomorrow’s teachers.

A leading provider of diverse, classroom-ready graduates with a passion to serve Colorado children.

Called to teach bilingual students? Need a master’s degree to advance in your career? Want to open your own school? Whether you’re a first-generation student interested in early childhood education or a veteran going back to school to teach physical education, you’ll get to the head of the class at MSU Denver.

MSU Denver is where learners connect with leaders.

99% of our classes are taught by faculty, not teaching assistants. They are industry icons, national experts and have the highest degrees in their disciplines.

Learn to fight crime from the former director of the NCIS. Analyze graphic novels with the convention director of Denver’s Pop Culture Con. Get bitten by the insect bug with a renowned mosquito biologist. Our professors are experts in their fields, but even better at passing on their expertise to students.
