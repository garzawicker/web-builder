Get experience in school

There are a variety of resources on campus that can help students get jobs and experience.

Check out the following offices for more information:

  • The MSU Denver Human Resources Office posts part-time on-campus student positions. You can earn money and get work experience without leaving campus.
  • The Applied Learning Center's Internship Program posts academic internships for current students on a database called "The Internship Program (TIP) Online." You can do internships for academic credit, pay or both depending upon departmental policies.
  • Career Services is the home of JobLink. JobLink posts off-campus part-time jobs for students as well as career positions for graduates.
