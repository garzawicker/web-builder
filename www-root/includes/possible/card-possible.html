What’s possible when you reimagine higher education?

A thriving urban university that fosters student success through classroom-to-career connections, relevant degrees and excellent faculty.

Build and operate satellites at an innovative aerospace company. Make beer at a legendary brewery. Produce an Emmy-winning newscast. Without leaving campus. That’s possible at Metropolitan State University of Denver.

Based in the heart of the Mile High City, MSU Denver provides unparalleled access to internships and professional networks that jump-start careers. Just ask this “SportsCenter” anchor. Professors with experience in their industries? We’ve got those, too: the former director of the NCIS, the convention director of Denver’s Comic Con and a biologist who got bitten by the mosquito bug.

The possibilities are endless.

MSU Denver is where learners connect with leaders.

99% of our classes are taught by faculty, not teaching assistants. They are industry icons, national experts and have the highest degrees in their disciplines.

Learn to fight crime from the former director of the NCIS. Analyze graphic novels with the convention director of Denver’s Comic Con. Get bitten by the insect bug with a renowned mosquito biologist.

That’s possible at Metropolitan State University of Denver.

Our professors are experts in their fields, but even better at passing on their expertise to students.

How? Hands-on learning built into every academic program. Whether it’s tracking storms outside the city, painting murals for local nonprofits or 3-D printing parts for Colorado space companies.

And learning from industry icons isn’t limited to the classroom. In the last year, we’ve had former presidents, media personalities and literary luminaries on campus to share their knowledge.
