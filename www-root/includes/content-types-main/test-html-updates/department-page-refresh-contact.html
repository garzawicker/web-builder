Marissa Molina

Trustee

Marissa Molina was appointed to the MSU Denver Board of Trustees in 2019.

She is the chair of the Governance Committee. Marissa also serves on the Finance Committee and is the MSU Denver representative to the MSU Denver Foundation Board.
